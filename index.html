FAQs & Policies

We would like to invite you to take a few minutes to read our hotel policies.
Moreover, we included in this section an advisory to guests that will be updated from time to time.

Reservation

  • Reservation: Confirmation of reservation is upon full payment of the total room charges. We'll send you our account details for bank transfer payments through SMS or email.
  • Cancellation Policies: All bookings are non refundable, non transferable and not convertible to other services, and considered guaranteed once finalized and paid. Cutting the number of nights-stay as indicated in the voucher will also be treated as equal. Any form of payment made without the signature of conformity signifies acceptance of the abovementioned terms and conditions.
  • All booking that falls on holidays and peak season (declared by the hotel), long weekends and cutting of the number of nights-stay as indicated in the voucher are non-refundable, non-transferable and not convertible to other services and considered guaranteed once finalized and paid.

Frontdesk

  • Please fill up the registration slip upon check-in.
  • Full payment is required for overnight guests.
  • If you would like to extend your stay, you are required to update your payments. All payments should be settled with the front desk clerk.
  • Please drop your room key at the front desk whenever you leave the room.
  • Daily check in time is 2:00 pm onwards. For early check-ins, an additional Php 100.00 per hour shall be charrged based on the 2 pm check-in time. Early check in starts 6:00 AM
  • Guest MUST check out by 12:00 noon. Please advise out staff at least one hour before checking out to facilitate settlement of accounts.

Use of Rooms

  • The room is for the exclusive use of registered guests. For comfort, the normal room capacity shall be strictly observed. Additional guests may be allowed subject to payment of fee for overnight stay which include set up of bed and use of hotel facilities.
  • Bringing in of any food and beverage in the room is strictly prohibited.
  • For safety reasons, the use of any cooking gadgets and electrical heating elements inside the hotel rooms are strictly prohibited.
  • Strictly no smoking inside the bedroom.
  • No alteration of room set up is allowed. Any hotel property damaged or lost due to act of negligence of guests shall be charged to the guests' account.
  • Housekeeping hours are 8:00 AM to 8:00 PM

Use of Swimming Pool

  • The swimming pool is for the exclusive use of registered guests only.
  • The use of proper swim gear must be strictly observed:
    • Swimsuit/bathing suit for girls and women.
    • Trunks/polyester shorts for boys and men.
  • Take a shower and thoroughly rinse off sand before swimming.
  • Do not dive into the swimming pool.
  • Guests under the influence of alcohol are not allowed to use the pool.
  • Do not drink and eat in the pool area.
  • Observe the swimming hours: 9:00 A.M. - 8:00 P.M.
